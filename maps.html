The Beautiful Blue Ridge Parkway
  • i

This map, created as a Final Semester Project, was a depiction of the Blue Ridge Parkway which is a road that traverses through the beautiful Blue Ridge Mountains in Virginia and North Carolina. This map highlights the route and the points of interest along the way. The route is oriented as the central focal point of the map with the surrounding state boundaries, water features, other roads, and topgraphy as reference.

Last, it finishes talking about the progress these laws have made but also the pushback that has resulted the overturning of these harmful laws.

The Roads of War: Riding the Civil War South
  • i

The map was created for a lab in my Carto-visualization class. This map was made in response to the prompt that we must create a bike route with a theme which traveled about 100 miles daily for 21 days. In this map, the bikers will travel throughout the southern United States to multiple important Civil War battlefields to learn more about the war. It starts where the Civil War began: Ft. Sumter, SC and end where the War ended: Appomattox, VA.
